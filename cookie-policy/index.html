What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience. Cookies help websites remember your preferences, settings, and browsing behavior.

Why we use cookies

At Oxyness, we use cookies to:

  • Remember your language preference so you don't have to select it every time
  • Remember your theme preference (light or dark mode) for a comfortable browsing experience
  • Store your cookie consent choices to respect your privacy preferences
  • Maintain your browsing session for security and functionality
  • Improve our website by understanding how visitors use it (with your consent)

Cookie categories

We categorize cookies based on their purpose and ask for your consent where required by GDPR regulations.

Necessary Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies as they are strictly necessary for providing services you have requested.

Examples: Session management, cookie consent storage

Functional Cookies

Requires Consent

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your language preference or theme choice. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we use on our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, some or all of these services may not function properly.

Examples: Language preference, theme preference

Analytics Cookies

Requires Consent

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us understand which pages are most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous.

Examples: Google Analytics (planned, not yet implemented)

Not currently in use

Marketing/Advertising Cookies

Requires Consent

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device.

Examples: Social media pixels, advertising networks

Not currently in use

How to manage your cookie preferences

You have full control over which cookies we use. Here's how you can manage your preferences:

Cookie Settings on Oxyness

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the 'Cookie Settings' button in the footer of any page. This will open our cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept or reject all cookies at once
  • Customize your preferences by category (Necessary, Functional, Analytics, Marketing)
  • View detailed information about each cookie category
  • Save your preferences and apply them immediately

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Note: Please note that blocking all cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.

Your rights and privacy

Your privacy is important to us. Under GDPR regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed: You have the right to clear, transparent information about how we use cookies (this policy)
  • Right to control: You have the right to choose which non-essential cookies to accept or reject
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent for functional, analytics, or marketing cookies at any time through Cookie Settings
  • Right to access: You can request information about what data is stored in cookies

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy. Privacy Policy.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
