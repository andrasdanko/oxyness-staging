Within the sprawling digital ecosystem of esoteric services, Astroyogi emerges as a venerable institution—India's oldest continuously operating online astrology platform, established in 2001 through the vision of founders Meena Bahl Kapoor and Aditya Kapoor. This is not merely a consultation service; it functions as a comprehensive spiritual infrastructure connecting seekers with over 5,000 verified practitioners spanning astrology, tarot, numerology, and Vastu Shastra across 173 countries. Beyond the immediacy of 24/7 consultations accessible through chat, call, or the Yogi Live video interface, the platform cultivates an extensive repository of free divinatory resources: daily horoscopes that track celestial rhythms, kundli generation that maps natal configurations, matchmaking algorithms that assess astrological compatibility, and yearly predictive frameworks. Their material extension—Yogii by Astroyogi—operates as a curated marketplace for lab-certified gemstones, rudraksha beads calibrated to specific energetic frequencies, and spiritual implements backed by a 25-year lineage of authenticity verification. With 30 million registered participants, entry-level consultation rates beginning at Rs 9 per minute, and a constellation of 6 specialized mobile applications, Astroyogi has positioned itself as the archetypal platform synthesizing traditional Eastern metaphysical systems with contemporary technological infrastructure and emergent AI integration.

History & Background

Founded
2001
Founders
Meena Bahl Kapoor and Aditya Kapoor
Company
Netway India Private Limited (also registered as PunditJunction Services Private Limited)

Gurugram, Haryana, India

Astroyogi was inaugurated on January 24th, 2001 by Meena Bahl Kapoor, positioning it among the pioneering architects of online astrological services within India. The platform initially manifested as a content creation entity for telecom partnerships with industry titans including Microsoft Network (MSN), Yahoo, and AOL, establishing expertise in digital content distribution for mass audiences. As the mobile revolution accelerated, Astroyogi pivoted toward the value-added services segment for telecommunications corporations, concentrating on content creation, curation, and live service delivery. This B2B experience established foundational understanding of scale and quality control in spiritual content distribution. The pivotal metamorphosis arrived in 2016 when Aditya Kapoor joined as co-founder. Recognizing substantial unmet demand within the astrotech domain, they launched India's inaugural dedicated astrology application with emphasis on privacy architecture and practitioner quality verification. This app-first strategy proved prescient, transforming Astroyogi from a content provider to a comprehensive astrotech platform. A key differentiator manifests in the 'Yogi Oath'—analogous to the Hippocratic Oath in medical practice—which every practitioner must pledge, ensuring ethical conduct and responsible guidance while combating industry skepticism around credibility and fear-mongering tactics. Under CEO Meena Bahl Kapoor's sustained leadership, the company has expanded beyond consultations into e-commerce with their Yogii retail segment, distributing authenticated spiritual implements from agarbattis to premium gemstones. In FY24, Astroyogi surpassed Rs 85.3 crore in revenue while maintaining bootstrapped (unfunded) status, and has established ambitious targets of Rs 250 crore within two years through AI integration and spiritual retail expansion. Their vision: "To become the world's most trusted platform for spiritual connection, blending soul, software, and social impact into one seamless ecosystem."
