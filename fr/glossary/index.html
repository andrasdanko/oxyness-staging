Glossaire

Explorez les termes spirituels et ésotériques

Chakra

Energy centers in the body according to ancient Indian traditions, believed to regulate physical, mental, and spiritual ...

Karma

The spiritual principle of cause and effect where intent and actions influence future outcomes.

Meditation

A practice of focused attention and mindfulness to achieve mental clarity and emotional calmness.

Astral Projection

An intentional out-of-body experience where consciousness separates from the physical body.

Aura

The subtle energy field believed to surround living beings, reflecting their emotional and spiritual state.

Enlightenment

A state of profound spiritual awakening and understanding of ultimate reality.

Mantra

A sacred word, sound, or phrase repeated during meditation or spiritual practice.

Nirvana

The ultimate state of liberation from suffering and the cycle of rebirth in Buddhism.

Reincarnation

The belief that the soul or consciousness is reborn into new bodies after death.

Third Eye

The sixth chakra located between the eyebrows, associated with intuition and spiritual insight.

Zen

A school of Mahayana Buddhism emphasizing meditation and intuition over theoretical knowledge.

Kundalini

Dormant spiritual energy believed to reside at the base of the spine, symbolized as a coiled serpent.

Mindfulness

The practice of maintaining present-moment awareness without judgment.

Tantra

Ancient spiritual traditions that use rituals, meditation, and energy work for spiritual transformation.

Yoga

A spiritual and physical practice originating in ancient India, aimed at uniting body, mind, and spirit.
