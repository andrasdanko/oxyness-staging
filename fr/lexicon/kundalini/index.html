Kundalini

Définition

Dormant spiritual energy believed to reside at the base of the spine, symbolized as a coiled serpent.

Explication Détaillée

Kundalini is a concept in yoga and tantra representing primal energy. When awakened through spiritual practices, it rises through the chakras along the spine, leading to spiritual enlightenment and transformation. Kundalini awakening can be intense and is sometimes accompanied by physical sensations, emotional releases, and altered states of consciousness.
