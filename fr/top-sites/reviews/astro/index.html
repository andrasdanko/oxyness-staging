Astrodienst AG was established in Switzerland by Dr. Alois Treindl, who developed the revolutionary Swiss Ephemeris astronomical calculation engine in 1997. The Swiss Ephemeris, built upon NASA's JPL DE405/406 data, provided unprecedented accuracy in planetary position calculations and quickly became the industry standard for astrological software worldwide. Under Dr. Treindl's leadership as CEO and webmaster, Astro.com evolved into "the first address for astrology on the web." The company's breakthrough came through partnerships with legendary astrologers, most notably Liz Greene, whose psychologically sophisticated interpretations set a new standard for computer-generated horoscopes. The proprietary Astro*Intelligence technology enables the software to synthesize chart factors the way professional astrologers do, recognizing inner patterns and personality structures. Today, Astrodienst maintains its headquarters in Zollikon near Z√ºrich, operating with a dedicated team including company manager and astrologer Juri Viktor Stork and editor Karin Hoffmann. The company continues to develop its ephemeris (updated to JPL DE431 in 2014) while expanding its content library with contributions from internationally respected authors including Robert Hand, Markus Jehle, and Bernadette Brady.
