Aura

Meghatározás

Az élőlényeket körülvevő finom energiamező, amely tükrözi érzelmi és spirituális állapotukat.

Részletes Magyarázat

Many spiritual traditions describe the aura as layers of colored light surrounding the body. Different colors are thought to correspond to various emotional states, personality traits, and levels of spiritual development. Some people claim to see auras, while others use photography techniques like Kirlian photography to capture them.
