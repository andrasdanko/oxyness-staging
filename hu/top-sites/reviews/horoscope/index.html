Horoscope.com operates as the flagship website of Horoscope.com, Inc., which functions as the media division of Ingenio LLC, a San Francisco-based company. The site has established itself as a premier destination for free horoscope content, combining entertainment with astrological guidance. The platform's mission centers on helping users on their path to self-empowerment through a comprehensive library of tarot readings and games that provide space for users to ask their most burning questions about relationships, career, and life direction. The team describes themselves as humble, operating with a small group of in-house editors supported by a network of freelancers. Their stated values include acting like owners, maintaining commitment to high-quality content, and challenging limiting beliefs in their exploration of astrology. As part of the Ingenio LLC family, Horoscope.com connects to a broader network of affiliated companies operating a worldwide portfolio of brands, enabling data sharing for group operations, marketing purposes, and service improvements across platforms.
