Kasamba has operated as an online psychic marketplace for over two decades, establishing itself as one of the longest-running platforms in the digital divination space. The platform emerged during the early 2000s internet boom and has maintained continuous operation since. The company operates as Kasamba Inc. headquartered in San Francisco, California, with international operations managed through adviqo GmbH in Berlin, Germany (EU Representative) and adviqo UK Limited in Norwich (UK Representative). This multi-jurisdictional corporate structure reflects the platform's global reach and regulatory compliance across the United States, European Union, and United Kingdom. Throughout its history, Kasamba has accumulated over 4 million five-star ratings and reviews, serving millions of clients globally. The platform has evolved from early web-based chat services to include mobile applications, voice calling, and sophisticated advisor matching systems while maintaining its core marketplace model connecting seekers with spiritual advisors.

Our Recommendation

Kasamba stands as one of the most established online psychic marketplaces, offering a legitimate platform for those seeking spiritual guidance, divination services, and intuitive readings. With over 20 years of operation and a stringent advisor approval process accepting less than 10% of applicants, the platform prioritizes quality practitioners over quantity. The platform excels for users seeking variety and convenience: 24/7 availability across 24+ reading categories, 500+ verified advisors, and flexible communication through chat or phone. The 3-minute free trial with multiple advisors provides genuine opportunity to find compatible practitioners before financial commitment. Substantial free content including daily horoscopes and educational articles adds value for casual visitors. Budget-conscious users should approach with caution. Per-minute pricing from $1.99 to $39.99 can accumulate rapidly during meaningful sessions, and the credit card requirement for free trials may deter privacy-focused individuals. The entertainment disclaimer limiting platform accountability for advice accuracy is standard industry practice but worth acknowledging. For those seeking spiritual insights with reasonable expectations, Kasamba offers a reliable, feature-rich marketplace backed by longevity and demonstrated user satisfaction. The platform merits a 7.5/10 rating—strong on features and selection, moderate on value proposition and trust factors given industry-standard disclaimers.
