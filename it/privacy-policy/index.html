Introduction

At Oxyness, we understand that your spiritual journey is deeply personal. We are committed to protecting your privacy while you explore spiritual wisdom, connect with advisors, and discover new resources. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights to control your personal data.

Your privacy matters to us. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

What data we collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with our website:

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit Oxyness, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device information (screen size, device type)
  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Referrer URL (where you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Cookie data (see our Cookie Policy for details)

User-Provided Data

Currently, Oxyness does not require user accounts or registration. However, in the future we may collect:

  • Contact information (email, name) if you contact us
  • Newsletter subscription email (planned feature)
  • User account information (planned feature)
  • Reviews and ratings you provide (planned feature)

We will update this policy and request your consent before implementing any of these features.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our website:

  • Search queries (to improve search results)
  • Category and topic browsing history
  • Advisor profiles you view
  • Links you click (including affiliate links)
  • Language and theme preferences

This browsing data is private and is never shared with advisors or third parties. Advisors cannot see who viewed their profiles.

Website Functionality

To protect our website from abuse, spam, and malicious activity, and to maintain the security of our systems.

Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest

Legal basis for processing

Under GDPR, we must have a legal basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

Legitimate Interest

We have a legitimate interest in operating our website, improving user experience, and protecting our systems from abuse. We balance this interest against your privacy rights.

Examples: Website functionality, security, error tracking

Consent

For non-essential cookies and analytics, we ask for your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent at any time through Cookie Settings.

Examples: Analytics cookies, marketing cookies (when implemented)

Legal Obligation

In some cases, we are required by law to process your personal data.

Examples: Responding to legal requests, tax compliance

Your privacy rights under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com

Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten')

You can request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com

Right to Restrict Processing

You can ask us to temporarily stop processing your personal data.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com

Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.

How to exercise: Contact us at privacy@oxyness.com or use Cookie Settings

Right to Withdraw Consent

For processing based on consent (cookies, analytics), you can withdraw consent at any time.

How to exercise: Use Cookie Settings in the footer or contact us

Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to file a complaint with your national data protection authority.

How to exercise: Find your authority at https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en

We will respond to all requests within 30 days as required by GDPR. There is no fee for exercising your rights unless your request is clearly unfounded or excessive.

How we protect your data

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure hosted in EU data centers
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Privacy by design in all new features

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we work hard to protect your data.

International data transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in the European Union:

Our hosting infrastructure is located in EU data centers, ensuring your data remains within the EU jurisdiction and subject to GDPR protections.

Exceptions

In the future, when we implement Google Analytics, some data may be transferred to the United States (Google's servers). This transfer will be protected by:

  • Your explicit consent (Analytics cookies must be accepted)
  • Google's GDPR-compliant data processing agreement
  • IP anonymization to reduce personal data exposure
  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the EU Commission

We will always inform you before implementing any services that transfer data outside the EU and ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Children's privacy

Oxyness is intended for adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 years of age. If you are under 16, please do not use this website or provide any personal information.

Note for Parents: If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@oxyness.com and we will delete the information.

Our business model and transparency

We believe in transparency about how we operate:

How we make money

Oxyness generates revenue through:

  • Affiliate links - When you visit advisor websites or purchase services through links on our site, we may earn a commission. This does not affect the price you pay.
  • Future revenue streams may include premium listings or advertising, which will be clearly marked and require your consent for cookies.

All advisor and website listings on Oxyness are currently free. We do not charge advisors to be listed in our directory.

If we introduce paid listings in the future, they will be clearly labeled to maintain transparency.

We do not sell your data

Your personal data is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties. Your privacy is not a product.

Spiritual content privacy

We understand that exploring spiritual and esoteric topics is personal. Your browsing history, searches, and the advisors you view are private. We do not share this information with advisors, other users, or third parties.

Automated decision-making and profiling

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you. In the future, we may use algorithms to recommend content or advisors based on your interests, but you will always have control over these features.
