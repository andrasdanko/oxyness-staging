History & Background

Founded
2001
Founders
Meena Bahl Kapoor and Aditya Kapoor
Company
Netway India Private Limited (also registered as PunditJunction Services Private Limited)
Headquarters
Gurugram, Haryana, India

Astroyogi was founded on January 24th, 2001 by Meena Bahl Kapoor, making it one of the pioneers of online astrology services in India. The platform initially emerged as a content creator for telecom partnerships with industry giants including Microsoft Network (MSN), Yahoo, and AOL, establishing expertise in digital content delivery for mass audiences. As the mobile revolution took hold, Astroyogi pivoted into the value-added services segment for telecom companies, focusing on content creation, curation, and live services. This B2B experience laid the foundation for understanding scale and quality control in spiritual content delivery. The pivotal transformation came in 2016 when Aditya Kapoor joined as co-founder. Recognizing substantial unmet demand in the astrotech space, they launched India's first dedicated astrology app with a focus on privacy and practitioner quality. This app-first strategy proved prescient, transforming Astroyogi from a content provider to a comprehensive astrotech platform. A key differentiator is the 'Yogi Oath' - similar to the Hippocratic Oath in medicine - which every astrologer must take, ensuring ethical conduct and responsible guidance while combating industry skepticism around credibility and fear-mongering tactics. Under CEO Meena Bahl Kapoor's continued leadership, the company has expanded beyond consultations into e-commerce with their Yogii retail segment, selling authenticated spiritual products from agarbattis to premium gemstones. In FY24, Astroyogi surpassed Rs 85.3 crore in revenue while remaining bootstrapped (unfunded), and has set ambitious targets of Rs 250 crore within two years through AI integration and spiritual retail expansion. Their vision: "To become the world's most trusted platform for spiritual connection, blending soul, software, and social impact into one seamless ecosystem."
