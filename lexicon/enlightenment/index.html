Enlightenment

Definition

A state of profound spiritual awakening and understanding of ultimate reality.

Detailed Explanation

Enlightenment represents the highest spiritual goal in many Eastern traditions. It involves transcending the ego, realizing one's true nature, and experiencing unity with all existence. Different traditions describe this state differently: as Nirvana in Buddhism, Moksha in Hinduism, or Satori in Zen. The path to enlightenment typically involves meditation, ethical living, and spiritual practice.
