Third Eye

Definição

The sixth chakra located between the eyebrows, associated with intuition and spiritual insight.

Explicação Detalhada

Also known as the Ajna chakra, the third eye is considered the center of intuition, foresight, and spiritual perception. When activated or "opened," it is believed to enhance psychic abilities, inner vision, and connection to higher consciousness. Many spiritual practices focus on awakening the third eye through meditation, visualization, and energy work.
