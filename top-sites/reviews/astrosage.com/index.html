AstroSage was established in 2004 by Punit Pandey with the mission of making astrology accessible, credible, and useful for everyone. The company has been a pioneer in India's digital astrology space, achieving several technology firsts. In 2001, prior to the main platform launch, the team created "Mobile Kundli," described as India's first consumer mobile application. The 2004 launch introduced AstroSage Cloud, characterized as the first cloud service designed for consumers in India. The platform continued innovating with the 2018 launch of Bhrigoo.ai, positioned as the world's first AI-powered Vedic astrologer. This AI system has since answered over 10 crore (100 million) questions. Today, AstroSage claims the title of world's most visited astrology platform with 11+ million monthly users, 1.2+ million daily active users, and 70+ million Android app downloads. The platform reports 80% market share in three categories: online Kundli generation, horoscope matching for marriage, and astrology software for professionals. The platform hosts over 700,000 registered astrologers providing consultation services.
